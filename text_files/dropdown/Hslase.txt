<h4 class="hedertext"> Haile Selassie I 
Monarch</h4>
<p class="article-text">
Haile Selassie I, born Tafari Makonnen Woldemikael, was Ethiopia's regent from 1916 to 1930 
and Emperor of Ethiopia from 1930 to 1974. He was the heir to a dynasty that traced its 
origins by tradition from King Solomon and Queen Makeda, Empress of Axum, known in the Abrahamic
 tradition as the Queen of Sheba.Haile Selassie is a defining figure in both
 </p>
